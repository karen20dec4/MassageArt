Q: What is a tantra-inspired therapeutic massage?

It is a holistic and mindful massage practice that uses principles from Tantra to promote deep relaxation, body awareness, and energetic balance. Unlike a conventional massage, it focuses on the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—in a meditative and respectful environment.



Q: Is this a sexual service?

Absolutely not. Our services are strictly professional and therapeutic. We do not offer any form of sexual service. Our practice honors the body as a temple and focuses on healing, relaxation, and mindfulness within clear ethical boundaries. Any inappropriate requests will result in the immediate termination of the session.



Q: What should I expect during my first session?

Each session begins with a calm conversation to discuss your intentions and establish a sense of trust and safety. The massage itself is a slow, meditative journey with mindful touch, designed to help you relax deeply and reconnect with your body at your own pace.



Q: I feel nervous or shy. Is that normal?

Yes, it is completely normal, especially for first-time clients. Our studio is a safe, welcoming, and non-judgmental space. We encourage you to share any concerns. Your comfort and peace of mind are our highest priorities.



Q: Who is this type of massage for?

Our therapeutic massage is for anyone seeking to reduce stress, deepen their mind-body connection, or explore a more mindful approach to wellness. We offer sessions for men, women, and couples, and each experience is tailored to the individual's needs.



Q: Can couples have a session together?

Yes, we offer sessions for couples. It is a beautiful way to deepen your connection and share a unique experience of relaxation and presence together in a guided, therapeutic setting.



Q: What should I wear?

You can come as you are. We will discuss everything, including draping and comfort levels, during the initial consultation to ensure you feel completely safe and at ease throughout the session.



Q: Is my privacy respected?

Absolutely. All sessions and conversations are held in the strictest confidence. Your privacy and trust are fundamental to the therapeutic work we do.
